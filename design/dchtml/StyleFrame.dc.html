The happy medium
Keep the scale tiny — a min/maxed two-attribute build tops out near 20 / 20, requirements sit at 1–9. At that range Concept A never piles up: a fixed box, pips that wrap at 10 and shrink, and a printed number as the anchor.

If a wild build ever blew past ~30 we'd revisit denominated blocks — but the design's job is to make sure it doesn't.
